Our Services

We understand quality, and our primary goal is to provide first class products and service.

Commission an Artist

If we don't have what you want, or you you have a specific need, then submit your required specifications to commission an artisan to make a statue for you.

Supporting Grass Roots.

Shop for Buddhas support grass root artists. By ordering from this site, your funds go to the artists, to help them to continue the tradition passed down through generations.

Quality Guarantee

We know the artists, we know the industry, we know the need for quality and vet all works that are for sale on the site. We guarantee that you will receive items of the finest quality.

Dharma Quality

We supply our products to Dharma practitioners around the world. We can supply for individuals, or for organisations. Feel free to contact us if you have enquiries. We would be happy to provide more information.

Hand Crafted

The work of the artisan cannot be replicated by automatic processes. The crafting of statues for Vajrayana practice has been the same for the past 1800 years or so. This process is requires patience, attention to detail, and each piece is unique.

From the source

If we don't have it, we can source it. With extensive connections in Nepal, we can source products of quality. Our interest is in supporting and preserving the crafts that have been maintained for generations.
