Hand crafted products made in Nepal by artisans in the same way since ancient times.

Fine dharma quality Buddha
statues

Hand crafted in Nepal using techniques passed down through generations since the second century AD.

Using an acient technique called the lost wax method, the each statue is individually crafted. A quality piece can take hundreds of hours to produce, and each piece is unique.

The pieces sold in Shop for Buddhas are of the highest quality, and support the craftsmen to continue this valuable tradition.

Qualtiy malas for meditaion.

Our quality malas are hand made in Nepal, with Buddist practitioners in mind. A range of bead types, bead sizes, to choose from.

If we don't have what your looking for, we can source it, or can make it to order.

Bodhi seed malas, once easily found and in plentiful supply have become more rare and expensive in recent years due to high demand. The price now for small bodhi seed malas is now about twenty times the price paid five to 10 years ago.

These items are now rare, and we can supply them, but per individual request.
